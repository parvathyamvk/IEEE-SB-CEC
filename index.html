IEEE

IEEE stands for the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. It is a professional association dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE is one of the world's largest technical professional organizations, with members from various fields of engineering, computer science, information technology, and related disciplines.

Vision

IEEE envisions a world where technology advances serve the greater good of humanity. The organization aims to be at the forefront of technological innovation, driving progress that enhances quality of life globally. IEEE strives to be a leader in promoting the responsible development and application of technology to address the challenges facing society.

Mission

The mission of IEEE is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. IEEE seeks to empower and connect technology professionals worldwide by providing resources, opportunities, and a collaborative platform. The organization is committed to inspiring a global community to work together on innovative solutions that create a better tomorrow.

CEC

The Government College of Engineering Chengannur (established in 1993), commonly known as CEC, is an engineering institute in the state of Kerala, India. The college was set up under the auspices of the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD) and is affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU). It currently offers undergraduate degrees in four engineering streams and two postgraduate programs.

About Us

We are leading global organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. Founded in 1963, IEEE has grown to become one of the world's largest technical professional organizations, with over 400,000 members in more than 160 countries.

Our mission is to foster technological innovation and excellence through our extensive range of professional and educational activities. IEEE supports a diverse range of fields including electrical engineering, computer science, and information technology. We provide a platform for professionals to collaborate, share knowledge, and advance their careers through conferences, publications, and technical standards.

At IEEE, we are committed to addressing global challenges and improving lives through technology. Our members contribute to groundbreaking research, develop standards that shape the future, and engage in community outreach to inspire the next generation of engineers and technologists.
